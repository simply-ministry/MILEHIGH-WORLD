# SOC 2 Access Control Policy

## 1. Objective
To ensure that only authorized individuals have access to information systems.

## 2. Joiner-Mover-Leaver Process
- **Joiner:** Provisioning requires manager approval.
- **Mover:** Access rights are reviewed and adjusted upon role change.
- **Leaver:** Access is revoked within 24 hours of termination.

## 3. Authentication
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all production systems.
- Password complexity requirements are enforced.
