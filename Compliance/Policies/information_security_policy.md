# SOC 2 Information Security Policy

## 1. Objective
To establish a framework for protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our information assets.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party partners.

## 3. Key Controls
- **Access Control (CC6.1):** Access is granted based on the principle of least privilege.
- **Data Encryption (CC6.6):** Data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
- **Incident Response (CC7.3):** All security incidents must be reported and investigated.
- **Change Management (CC8.1):** All code changes must undergo peer review.
